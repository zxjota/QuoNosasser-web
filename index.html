Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 13, 2026

QuoNosasser (“we”, “our”, or “us”) provides a local password generation service through our mobile application and H5 pages. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. QuoNosasser does not upload, sync, or transmit your passwords or personal information to any server or third party.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not require account registration, login, or internet connection. We do not collect location data, contact lists, or browsing history.

3. Password Generation

All passwords are generated locally on your device using built-in algorithms. No password data ever leaves your phone.

4. Permissions Used

  • Clipboard Access: To copy generated passwords to your clipboard (optional).

These permissions are used strictly for app functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

QuoNosasser does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or trackers. No user behavior data is shared externally.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Security

Since all information remains on your device, your privacy is protected by your device’s security settings. We recommend enabling screen lock or biometric authentication.
